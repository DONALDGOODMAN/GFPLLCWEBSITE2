02 / METHOD

A disciplined process

Commissioning is a structured methodology — not a checklist. Each phase produces documented evidence that systems perform to the owner's project requirements.

PH.01

Assess

Define owner's project requirements and review the basis of design against intent.

PH.02

Design Review

Examine drawings and specifications for performance, constructability, and gaps.

PH.03

Functional Testing

Witness and document system performance across the full range of operating modes.

PH.04

Verification

Confirm installed systems meet design intent; resolve and re-test deficiencies.

PH.05

Optimize & Report

Tune for efficiency and deliver a complete commissioning record and turnover.
